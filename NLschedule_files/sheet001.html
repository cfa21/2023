the Pittsburgh Pirates (PIT) and Miami Marlins (MIA) for two three-game series with a day of rest between. Trades and pickups made
in any Inning are effective at the end of the Inning. An owner who makes a transaction should report the Inning's stats with the old 
database, then make the transaction in the database and construct a new manager profile. The trading deadline is the end of the 
Sixth Inning. Callups/demotions are between Innings. Pitchers on the road half of the schedule must rest at least three games 
between starts, regardless of rest or Inning breaks; road rotations may be restarted at the All-Star Break (after Inning 4).
