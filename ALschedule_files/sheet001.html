Orioles then visit the Washington Nationals (WAS) and Boston Red Sox (BOS) for two three-game series with a day of rest between. 
Trades and pickups made in any Inning are effective after the Inning. An owner who makes a transaction should report the Inning's 
stats with the old database, then make the transaction in the database and construct a new manager profile. The trading deadline is
the end of the Sixth Inning. Callups/demotions are between Innings. Pitchers on the road half of the schedule must rest at least three
games between starts, regardless of rest or Inning breaks; road rotations may be restarted at the All-Star Break (after Inning 4).
